What is Aston Martin

Aston Martin is a renowned British luxury car manufacturer with a rich heritage.

Today, Aston Martin stands at the forefront of luxury automotive craftsmanship, combining cutting-edge technology with timeless design to produce some of the most desirable cars on the market. Some of our victories:

  • 24 Hours of Le Mans
  • FIA World Endurance Championship
  • GT Racing Championships

Aston Martin continues to maintain a strong global presence, with showrooms and dealerships in key markets around the world. The brand's reputation for luxury, performance, and exclusivity ensures a loyal customer base and a continued legacy as one of the most iconic automotive manufacturers in the world

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Aston Martin known for?

    Aston Martin is renowned for producing luxury sports cars with a blend of British craftsmanship, performance, and elegance. They're often associated with James Bond films, as many of the movies feature Aston Martin vehicles

  • Where is Aston Martin based?

    Aston Martin is headquartered in Gaydon, Warwickshire, England. They have manufacturing facilities in various locations, including Gaydon, Newport Pagnell, and St Athan.

  • When was Aston Martin founded?

    Aston Martin was founded in 1913 by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford. The company initially started as Bamford & Martin Limited, later becoming Aston Martin in recognition of Lionel Martin's successful performances at the Aston Hill Climb.

  • Does Aston Martin only produce sports cars?

    While sports cars are the core of Aston Martin's lineup, they have also ventured into producing luxury grand tourers and limited-edition hypercars. However, their primary focus remains on high-performance vehicles.

  • Who owns Aston Martin?

    Aston Martin Lagonda Global Holdings plc is a publicly traded company listed on the London Stock Exchange. Various investors and shareholders own stakes in the company.

  • Does Aston Martin offer customization options for buyers?

    Yes, Aston Martin provides extensive customization options for customers through their "Q by Aston Martin" program. This allows buyers to tailor various aspects of their vehicle, including exterior colors, interior trims, and personalized features, to suit their preferences..
